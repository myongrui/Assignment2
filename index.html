What exactly is Covid-19 and how does it spread

Otherwise known as Coronavirus disease, is an infectious disease caused by the SARS-CoV-2 Virus. Infected people will experience mild to moderate respiratory illness and can recover without any special treatment. The virus can spreads through the infected person's mouth or nose in small liquid particles.

Origin of Covid-19 Virus

Thought to be origined from animals, it is most likely a spillover infection (spread from one species to another) from bats to humans. Analytics have shown that it may have already been circulating in Wuhan before Wuhan where the first human infections were confirmed.

Timeline of Covid-19

The earliest reported onset of symptoms was on 1 December 2019, on 18 December 2019, traces of the virus have been found in wastewater samples were collected in Milan and Turin,Italy. In 20 January 2020, first human-to-human transmission was reported by WHO and Chinese authorities. On 30 January, the WHO declared COVID-19 a Public Health Emergency of International Concern in which by then, the outbreak spread by a factor of 100 to 200 times.
